Job Seekers Site Plan

Connecting People with Opportunities

Author: Jones Makafui Awuah

1. Site Name

Job Seekers

The name Job Seekers represents a platform dedicated to helping individuals find employment opportunities that match their skills, goals, and interests. It reflects both simplicity and purpose — connecting people with meaningful work.

Optional domain: jobseekershub.org

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a user-friendly hub for job seekers to explore job listings, career resources, and resume-building tools. It aims to:

  • Provide current job opportunities across multiple industries
  • Offer resources for career development and interview preparation
  • Allow users to submit applications or contact recruiters directly
  • Support users through helpful guides and professional tips

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I find the latest job listings in my field?
  • How can I contact recruiters or submit my application?
  • What tips and tools are available to improve my resume and interview skills?

4. Color Schema

The Job Seekers color palette combines professional and modern tones for clarity and trust.

  • Primary Color: #0c4b78 – used for headers, nav bar, and accents
  • Secondary Color: #0a3d66 – used for footer and dark backgrounds
  • Accent Color: #00a5cf – used for buttons, icons, and links
  • Muted Gray: #6b7a86 – used for secondary text
  • White: #ffffff – used for cards and main backgrounds

5. Typography

The main font family is “Segoe UI”, Roboto, Arial, sans-serif — clean and professional for readability.

  • Heading Font: Segoe UI — used for titles, navigation links, and section headings.
  • Body Font: Roboto — used for body paragraphs and descriptive text.
