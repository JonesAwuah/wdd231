Highlighting Your Skills Effectively

Your skills section helps hiring managers quickly assess whether you have the capabilities they’re looking for. A great skills section balances technical proficiency with soft skills that demonstrate teamwork, leadership, and adaptability.

Types of Skills to Include:

  • Hard Skills: Specific, teachable abilities like data analysis, coding, accounting, or design.
  • Soft Skills: Interpersonal qualities like communication, leadership, or problem-solving.
  • Transferable Skills: Skills applicable across multiple roles, such as project management or critical thinking.
Example of a Balanced Skills Section:
Technical Skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Excel, Google Analytics
Soft Skills: Time Management, Team Collaboration, Critical Thinking

Tips for an Effective Skills Section:

  • Tailor your skills to match the job description.
  • Group related skills under headings (e.g., Technical, Communication, Management).
  • Use bullet points or columns for easy scanning.
  • Keep the list concise — 8–12 skills maximum.
