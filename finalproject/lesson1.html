Understanding the Purpose of a Resume

Your resume is your personal marketing tool — it introduces you to potential employers and showcases why you’re the best fit for a role. A strong resume not only lists your experience but tells your story of growth, skill, and success.

Key Principles:

  • Keep it clear and concise — most resumes should be one page long.
  • Focus on relevance — highlight skills and experiences that match the job.
  • Use professional formatting — make it easy to scan quickly.
  • Tailor your resume for each position you apply for.
Example:
Imagine two candidates applying for a marketing role. One uses a generic resume with unrelated experience, while the other highlights marketing achievements and related skills. The second candidate stands out immediately.
