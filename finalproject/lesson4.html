Highlighting Your Education the Right Way

The Education section of your resume is more than just a list of schools — it’s a chance to show your foundation of skills and commitment to learning. Whether you’re a student, recent graduate, or experienced professional, this section must reflect relevance and credibility.

Key Tips:

  • List your most recent education first.
  • Include your degree, institution, and graduation year.
  • Add honors, awards, or coursework if they relate to your target job.
  • If you lack work experience, place this section above work experience.
Example Format:
Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
University of Ghana, Legon — Graduated: 2023
- Relevant Coursework: Data Structures, Web Development, Software Engineering
- Awarded Dean’s Honor (2022)

Bonus Tip:

If you’re currently pursuing a degree, include your expected graduation date. This shows employers you’re actively learning and progressing.

Current Student Example:
Bachelor of Business Administration
KNUST, Kumasi — Expected Graduation: 2026
- Coursework: Marketing Strategy, Financial Analysis, Business Ethics
