Building a Strong Resume

Your resume is your first impression. Learn how to create a document that stands out to employers.

  • Use action verbs and measurable achievements.
  • Tailor your resume to each job description.
  • Keep formatting clean and easy to scan.
  • Highlight your most relevant experiences first.

Balancing your career and personal life is key to long-term success and happiness.
